1. 🚀 Getting Started

🔑 Login

  1. Open your browser and go to the app URL.
  2. Enter your email and password.
  3. Click Login.
💡 Default super admin: admin@retailpro.kw / admin123. Change this after first login.

🌐 Switch Language

Click the 🌐 English / Arabic button in the top bar. The entire interface switches instantly, including numbers and invoice printouts.

🧭 Navigation Menu

Use the left sidebar or the ☰ mobile menu:

2. 🏪 POS — Make a Sale

🖥️ Open POS

Click POS in the menu. The screen is split into two parts:

  • Left — Product search and catalog.
  • Right — Cart, customer, totals, and checkout.

👤 Select a Customer

  1. Click the customer tab. Choose Walk-in or Saved Customer.
  2. For a saved customer, type the name or phone in the search box. Results appear as you type.
  3. Click the customer to select.
💡 For wholesale customers, the POS automatically uses wholesale prices.

➕ Quick Add a New Customer

  1. Click the + Quick Add button in the customer area.
  2. Enter name, phone, and type (retail or wholesale).
  3. Click Save. The customer is selected immediately without losing the cart.

🔍 Find a Product

  1. Click the product search box and type the product name, SKU, or barcode.
  2. Click the product card to add it to the cart.
  3. Or use a barcode scanner — it automatically searches and adds the product.
💡 Only products with stock in your current branch will appear.

🛒 Add to Cart

  • Click a product once to add 1 unit.
  • Click the + / − buttons to change quantity.
  • Click the 🗑️ icon to remove an item.
  • Click the item to apply a manual discount.

📦 Sell by Box / Pair / Dozen

Some products support pack selling (e.g., a box of 12 chargers, a pair of earphones, a dozen cables).

  1. Click the product card.
  2. If the product is set as a pack, a popup asks: Unit or Box / Pair / Dozen.
  3. Choose the mode. The cart shows the pack label and adjusts the price.
💡 Selling one box deducts the pack size (e.g., 12 pieces) from stock.

🏷️ Apply a Discount

  1. Click the cart item.
  2. Enter the discount amount in KWD.
  3. Click Apply.

🎯 Apply an Offer / Promotion

  1. At the bottom of the cart, click the Offers dropdown.
  2. Select an active offer (e.g., Buy 2 iPhone cases, get 10% off).
  3. The discount is applied automatically on the server side.

⏸️ Hold a Cart

  1. Click the Hold button.
  2. Give the cart a name (e.g., customer name).
  3. The cart is saved in the browser. You can serve other customers.

▶️ Resume a Held Cart

  1. Click the Resume button.
  2. Select the held cart from the list.
  3. The cart is restored with customer and items.

💳 Checkout

  1. Review the cart, subtotal, discount, tax, and total.
  2. Choose payment mode: Cash, KNET, WAMD, Transfer, or Credit.
  3. For credit or partial payment, enter the amount paid.
  4. Click Complete Sale.
  5. The invoice prints automatically or opens the print page.
⚠️ Credit sales will increase the customer’s outstanding balance. Make sure they are allowed credit.

🧾 Print Receipt

  • The invoice print page opens after sale.
  • Click the browser print button or press Ctrl+P / Cmd+P.
  • For thermal receipt, set printer format to 80mm in Settings.

3. 🏷️ Products & Categories

➕ Add a Product

  1. Go to Products → click Add Product.
  2. Fill in:
    • Name (English)
    • Arabic Name (for Arabic invoices)
    • SKU — unique product code
    • Barcode — for scanner
    • Category
    • Brand, Color, Size
    • Cost Price — what you paid the supplier
    • Retail Price — normal selling price
    • Wholesale Price — for wholesale customers
    • Unit Type — piece, pair, dozen, box, kg, g, m
    • Pack Size — for box only (e.g., 12 pieces per box)
    • Initial Stock — adds stock to all branches when creating
  3. Click Save.

✏️ Edit / Delete a Product

  1. On the Products list, click the ✏️ edit icon.
  2. Change the fields you need.
  3. Click Update.
  4. To delete, click the 🗑️ icon. You can only delete products with no invoices.

📂 Add a Category

  1. Go to Categories.
  2. Enter category name and emoji (e.g., 📱 Mobile Phones).
  3. Click Save.
💡 Use emojis to make products easy to find in POS.

4. 🗄️ Inventory & Stock

📊 View Stock Levels

  1. Go to Inventory.
  2. Use the filters: branch, category, search.
  3. The table shows current quantity for each product per branch.
💡 Products with low stock (≤ 5) show a warning. The sidebar also shows the low-stock count.

➕ Stock IN / Stock OUT

  1. On the Inventory page, click Add Movement.
  2. Select type: Stock IN or Stock OUT.
  3. Select the branch.
  4. Type the product name in the search box and select it.
  5. Enter quantity and reference (e.g., manual adjustment).
  6. Click Save.

🔄 Transfer Stock Between Branches

  1. Click Add Movement.
  2. Select type: Transfer.
  3. Select Source Branch (e.g., Main Branch).
  4. Select Destination Branch.
  5. Select product and enter quantity.
  6. Click Save.
⚠️ You cannot transfer to the same branch. The source must have enough stock.

📝 Stock Movements History

Scroll down on the Inventory page. Filter by type (IN, OUT, Transfer, Damage, Return) to see all changes.

💔 Mark Damaged / Returned Stock

Use the same Add Movement form, but select type Damage or Return. The quantity will be deducted from stock.

5. 🏷️ Batches & Expiry

🧪 What Are Batches?

Batches track products with expiry dates, like SIM cards, batteries, or promotional items. Each batch has a number, supplier, expiry date, and quantity.

➕ Add a Batch

  1. Go to Batches & Expiry.
  2. Click Add Batch.
  3. Select product, supplier, branch.
  4. Enter batch number, expiry date, and quantity.
  5. Click Save.

🔔 Expiry Alerts

The sidebar shows a red badge when products are expiring within 90 days. Go to Batches to see which items expire soon.

📉 FIFO Selling

When you sell a batched product, the system automatically deducts from the oldest batch first.

6. 🏭 Suppliers & Purchases

➕ Add a Supplier

  1. Go to Suppliers.
  2. Click Add Supplier.
  3. Enter company name, contact person, phone, email, VAT number, payment terms, and address.
  4. Click Save.

🛒 Create a Purchase Order (PO)

  1. Go to Purchases.
  2. Click New Purchase Order.
  3. Select Supplier and Branch (where stock will be received).
  4. Add items:
    • Click Add Item.
    • Select product from search.
    • Enter quantity and unit cost.
    • For box products, enter pack size.
  5. Click Save PO.

📥 Receive Stock from PO

  1. In the Purchases list, find the PO.
  2. Click Mark Complete.
  3. The system adds all items to the branch stock automatically.
  4. Supplier balance increases (you now owe the supplier).

💰 Pay a Supplier

  1. Go to Payments.
  2. Select type Supplier.
  3. Choose the supplier.
  4. Enter amount and payment mode.
  5. Click Save.
💡 Supplier balance turns to 0 when fully paid. Negative balance means you owe them.

🖨️ Print PO

On the Purchases list, click the PO number to open the print page. Use browser print to save as PDF or print.

7. 👥 Customers

➕ Add a Customer

  1. Go to Customers.
  2. Click Add Customer.
  3. Enter name, company name, phone, email, address.
  4. Choose type: Retail or Wholesale.
  5. Set Credit Limit if they are allowed to buy on credit.
  6. Click Save.

🔍 Search Customers

Use the search box to filter by name, company, phone, or email. Use the balance filter to find customers who owe money.

💳 Customer Balance

BalanceMeaning
Negative (e.g., -50 KWD)Customer owes you money.
ZeroAll invoices are paid.
Positive (e.g., 50 KWD)Customer paid in advance.

💰 Receive Customer Payment

  1. Go to Payments.
  2. Select type Customer.
  3. Choose the customer.
  4. Enter amount and payment mode.
  5. Click Save.
💡 The system will automatically settle the oldest unpaid invoices first.

✏️ Edit / Delete Customer

  1. Click the ✏️ icon on the customer row.
  2. Update details and save.
  3. You can only delete customers with no invoices.

8. 📋 Quotations

➕ Create a Quotation

  1. Go to Quotations.
  2. Click New Quotation.
  3. Select customer and branch.
  4. Add products, quantities, and prices.
  5. Click Save.

🖨️ Print / Convert Quotation

  1. Open the quotation by clicking the quote number.
  2. Click Print to print or save as PDF.
  3. Click Convert to Invoice when the customer accepts the quote.
💡 Quotations do not affect stock until converted to an invoice.

9. 🧾 Invoices & Refunds

📜 View Invoices

  1. Go to Reports or click the invoice history link.
  2. Filter by date, branch, or customer.
  3. Click an invoice number to view details.

🖨️ Reprint an Invoice

  1. Click the invoice number.
  2. Click the 🖨️ print button or use browser print.

↩️ Process a Refund

  1. Go to Refunds.
  2. Enter the invoice number or search by customer.
  3. Select the invoice.
  4. Choose which items to return and enter return quantity.
  5. Choose refund mode: Return to stock or Do not return.
  6. Click Process Refund.
  7. The invoice is updated and stock is restored if selected.
⚠️ Refunds can only be done within the refund period set in Settings.

🗑️ Delete an Invoice

  1. Go to Reports.
  2. Find the invoice and click Delete.
  3. Confirm the deletion.
⚠️ Deleting an invoice returns all stock and reverses the customer balance. Only managers/admins can do this.

10. 📈 Reports

📊 Sales Reports

  1. Go to Reports.
  2. Select date range and branch.
  3. Click Generate.
  4. See total sales, collected, outstanding, gross profit, and invoice count.

💹 Profit & Loss

Click the Profit & Loss tab to see gross profit minus expenses for the selected period.

📁 Category Breakdown

Click the Category Breakdown tab to see which product categories sold the most.

📅 Daily Trend

Click the Daily Trend tab to see sales per day with a bar chart.

💳 Payment Modes

Click the Payment Modes tab to see how much was paid by cash, KNET, transfer, etc.

📥 Export to Excel

  1. Generate the report.
  2. Click the 📊 Excel button.
  3. A CSV file downloads and opens in Excel.

🖨️ Print Reports

Click the Print button. The report will be formatted for printing.

11. 💳 Payments

📝 Record a Payment

  1. Go to Payments.
  2. Select type: Customer or Supplier.
  3. Select the person/company.
  4. Enter amount and payment mode.
  5. Add notes if needed.
  6. Click Save.

🔍 Payment History

The Payments page shows all recorded payments. You can filter by type and search by name.

✏️ Edit / Delete a Payment

  1. Click the ✏️ or 🗑️ icon on the payment row.
  2. Delete will reverse the customer or supplier balance automatically.

💵 Customer Dues

The sidebar shows the number of customers who owe money. Click the 🔔 notification icon to go to Payments.

12. 💸 Expenses

➕ Add an Expense

  1. Go to Expenses.

14. 🔐 Users & Roles

➕ Add a User

  1. Go to Users.
  2. Click Add User.
  3. Enter name, email, password, role, and branch.
  4. Click Save.

👤 Assign Branch to Cashier

When adding a cashier, select their branch. They will only see their branch’s stock and invoices.

🚫 Deactivate a User

  1. Click the ✏️ icon.
  2. Uncheck Active.
  3. Click Update.

15. 🎯 Offers / Promotions

➕ Create an Offer

  1. Go to Offers.
  2. Click Add Offer.
  3. Enter offer name, discount type (percentage or fixed amount), and value.
  4. Set minimum quantity or applicable categories/products.
  5. Set start and end date.
  6. Click Save.

🛒 Use Offer in POS

  1. In POS, add qualifying products to the cart.
  2. Select the offer from the Offers dropdown.
  3. The discount is applied automatically.
💡 Discounts are recalculated on the server to prevent manipulation.

16. ⚙️ Settings

🏢 Company Settings

  1. Go to Settings → Company tab.
  2. Enter company name (English and Arabic), address, phone.
  3. Upload a company logo.
  4. Click Save.

🌍 Country / Tax Profile

  1. Go to Settings → Tax tab.
  2. Select a country profile (Kuwait, UAE, Saudi, etc.).
  3. Or manually set currency, tax type, tax rate, and VAT number.
  4. Click Save.

🧾 Invoice Settings

  1. Go to Settings → Invoice tab.
  2. Set invoice prefix (e.g., INV-).

  4. Choose printer format: A4 or 80mm Thermal.
  5. Set refund period days.
  6. Click Save.

🔒 Session Timeout

Users are automatically logged out after 2 hours of inactivity. This protects the system.

18. 💡 Tips & Troubleshooting

✅ Best Practices

  • Always select the correct customer before checkout.
  • Close the day by checking the daily sales report.
  • Check the low-stock badge in the sidebar every morning.
  • Record supplier payments immediately to keep balances accurate.
  • Do not delete invoices unless necessary; use refunds instead.

⚠️ Common Issues

IssueSolution
Product not found in POSCheck stock in the correct branch and that product is active.
Stock transfer failsMake sure source branch has enough stock.
Customer not showingMake sure the customer is active.
Session expiredLog in again. Your work was saved if you used Hold.
Invoice print looks wrongChange printer format in Settings → Invoice.

🆘 Need Help?

Contact your system administrator or developer. Check the logs folder if something is not working.
